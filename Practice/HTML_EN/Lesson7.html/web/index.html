Method: Keep flowers long

  • Change water of flower vase every day
  • Rinse the vase and pin before attaching
  • Avoid leaving the vase with sunshine, wind under the fan, on the windowsill
  • If the flower is dehydrated, use a spray bottle to spray the flowers
    Except flower and white daisies must spray the flower back
  • Choose fresh flower, dark colors, lush calyx, flower stalks are not broken
